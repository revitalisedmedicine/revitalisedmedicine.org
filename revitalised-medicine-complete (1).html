About us

Galvanising a student
community for medicine

Welcome to Revitalised Medicine ‚Äî your gateway to insightful medical articles, resources, and initiatives. We explore content related to recent medical research and informative publications across healthcare, including oncology, biotechnology, and genetics.

Our organisation aims to spread STEM-related knowledge about healthcare by galvanising a student community filled with passionate prospective medical providers. In 2025, we reached over 93,000 people through international media.

In the long term, we strive for revolutionary change throughout all communities, emphasising the importance of medical education accessibility and healthcare advocacy. Let's keep pushing to make an even bigger impact in 2026!

What we do
  • Publish peer-reviewed medical articles across 12+ specialties
  • Support student chapters at schools and community centres
  • Advocate for equitable healthcare access globally
  • Partner with organisations like MediQuills & CureQuest
  • Contribute EU health policy papers and position statements
  • Run webinars, diagnostics content, and a neurology library

How to Start a Chapter

Everything you need to launch a Revitalised Medicine chapter.

Starting a chapter is one of the most impactful ways to get involved. Whether at a school, university, or community centre, chapters bring medical education and health advocacy directly to local communities.

Step 1: Fill Out the Chapter Application

Complete our chapter application form. We review applications on a rolling basis and will be in touch within a few weeks.

Step 2: Recruit Your Team

Every strong chapter needs a core team. Look for students passionate about medicine, science, and health advocacy. Aim for at least 3‚Äì5 founding members.

Step 3: Get Institutional Support

Identify a faculty advisor or community sponsor at your institution who can provide logistical support.

Step 4: Plan Your First Initiative

Check out our initiative planning guide for ideas. A great first event could be a speaker talk, study group, or health awareness campaign.

Step 5: Connect with HQ

Once active, stay connected with the central Revitalised Medicine team for resources, publishing opportunities, and cross-chapter collaboration.

Ready to get started?

Organise Chapter Initiatives

Frameworks and ideas for running impactful events at your local chapter.

Revitalised Medicine chapters thrive when they run regular, engaging initiatives that educate and inspire their communities. Here are some proven formats to get you started.

Educational Events

Host speaker talks, panel discussions, or mini-lectures on topics from any department area. Invite local medical professionals, researchers, or fellow students to present.

Health Awareness Campaigns

Run campaigns on underrepresented health topics, particularly those relevant to rare diseases and marginalised communities. Use social media, posters, and local outreach.

Article Writing Workshops

Train chapter members to write and submit medical articles for publication on the Revitalised Medicine website. Partner with department editors to guide the process.

Community Partnerships

Partner with local hospitals, clinics, or nonprofits to provide volunteers, run health screenings, or distribute educational materials.

Webinars & Online Events

Collaborate with other chapters or the central team to host online webinars ‚Äî great for reaching a wider audience.
